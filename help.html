Plant
1.What’s on the main page?

You can plant (virtual) trees when you’re exercising! On the main page, from the top to bottom, you can see in how many minutes will you do your next exercise, your progress of planting your tree, and a button “Exercise Now” for you to use when you feel like doing some exercise immediately.


2. What’s about the profile page?

You can check your profile photo, number of trees planted, and kilometers walked. You can tap your profile picture or your name on the top left of the menu button to enter your profile page.


3.What is “My Forest”?

You aren’t just going to plant one tree aren’t you? Planting a tree requires several steps, so we value each tree you planted as your important memories. You can see the trees you’ve planted since you started to be a healthy guy, and the times you planted them. Track your memories here!


4.What is the time I’m setting in the “Time Setting”?

It’s the time interval for your exercise. In other words, it’s the time for “how long are you going to stand up and take a walk after sitting in front of the computer/television”. The default time you will get after you press “Reset” will be 1 hour.


5. Who are the guys on the leaderboard?

They are the guys who plant the most trees among all the users using this app. Don’t be stressed out and try to catch up with others by constantly going out to exercise of even faking the data. We just want you to have a good balance between working and exercising.
